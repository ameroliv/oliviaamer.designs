Author: Liv Amer

Project Context Overview

Engoo is an online language interface connecting students with professional tutors worldwide for personalised English lessons. The platform specialises in one-on-one video call lessons, catering to all proficiency levels. However, the platform currently faces challenges in providing a smooth user experience for tutor selection, leading to confusion and an overwhelming experience for students in their academic journey.

Defining the Key UX Problem

Project Topic:

Engoo is an Online language interface that allows students to connect with online professional tutors, offering personalised private English lessons from all around the world. It specialises in one-on-one lessons via video call, catering to learners on all levels, from beginning to advanced speakers. Their platform covers various aspects such as conversation practices, grammar and pronunciation.

What's the Issue?

Currently, Engoo fails to ensure there’s a smooth and user-friendly interaction when selecting a tutor . Due to information deficiency, users often find it challenging to make informed decisions about their tutors and where to begin. This leads to confusion and an overwhelming experience for students. The platform lacks personalised profiles , making the system ineffective for matching users with tutors based on personality, preferences, and learning needs. As a result, students must conduct manual research for each session, and tutors remain uncertain about lesson preparation. Additionally, this makes the website feel one-sided and less engaging for users.

Why This Context Matters

The definition of user experience refers to a user’s journey and perspective of an interaction. By analyzing Engoo’s ‘Our Tutor’ page from a student’s logged-in perspective, we can enhance the tutor selection process based on users’ needs, wants, and expectations.

This issue is crucial because tutors must understand students’ motives and needs to tailor their teaching effectively. Students also need to feel as if their profile authentically represents them and fosters a sense of connection to the server, making them feel part of a community. Addressing this will improve engagement and ease of use.

Secondary Research: Calendar & Bookings + Profile



Chow Bookings

Chow is a restaurant offering Fresh Asian Cuisine in Wellington. Their online booking system is efficient and user-friendly, guiding customers seamlessly through the reservation process. Engoo could benefit from adopting a similar approach to tutor scheduling, providing a smooth and intuitive booking system to enhance the user experience for both tutors and students.

Course Advisor Bookings: Victoria University

Victoria University’s course advisor booking system shares similarities with Chow’s approach, offering a simple and straightforward process for students to select a date and time with their advisor. Colour coordination helps students easily identify their selected booking day, and time clarity reduces confusion during the process.

Facebook Profile

Facebook profiles are personalised spaces where users can share their interests and experiences. This encourages engagement, similar to how Engoo should foster positive interactions between students and tutors. By allowing both parties to connect more deeply, Engoo can create a comfortable and engaging learning environment.

Design Brief

Identify UX Problem:

Engoo currently struggles to ensure a smooth and user-friendly experience when selecting a tutor. Users often feel overwhelmed by the large number of available tutors, making it difficult to make informed decisions.

Design Outcome:

Our design solution focuses on improving communication and organisation. By creating a more functional, usable, and scalable system, users will be able to navigate seamlessly, select dates, and find tutors that match their needs and preferences. The solution will feature an interactive flow with intuitive interfaces, clear visual cues, a progress bar, and personalised recommendations, leading to a more effective and enjoyable experience.

To Achieve This:

Personalized Profile: Engoo currently lacks a personal profile feature, which makes it difficult to match students with tutors based on personality, preferences, and learning needs. By incorporating personalised profiles right after signing in, we can offer a smooth transition into the system.

This profile will gather learning preferences, personality traits, and specific needs, helping the system suggest more accurate tutor matches.

Calendar System: The current calendar lacks clarity and usability. Users have reported frustration with the excessive empty space and unclear structure. To address this, we will implement a simple calendar interface with real-time availability updates, taking inspiration from the VUW course advisor system. The calendar will allow users to easily select dates and times for their tutor sessions in a streamlined manner.

Importance:

Building a schedule is crucial for design projects as it ensures each phase is completed on time and with structure. A well-organised schedule helps manage resources effectively and maintains focus throughout the project.

Additionally, incorporating user testing is vital throughout the process. Testing ensures the final design meets user expectations rather than the designer’s assumptions. By conducting 5 usability tests with clear scenarios, we will gather invaluable feedback on the user experience.
