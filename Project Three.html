Design Output Communication - Assignment 3



Title: Communicating Design Output: User Experience Showcase

Author: Liv Amer

Course: IXXN202 • Assignment Three

Objective: Distill and communicate design work through video format, focusing on key components such as the problem, design goals, output, and decision basis.



Revisiting Project Brief - Assignment Three

In this assignment, I will communicate my design output to a general audience through a 3-5 minute video, focusing on clearly explaining the problem, the design goals and why they are important, the design output, and the basis for my design decisions. The video will serve as a portfolio piece, showcasing my ability to distill complex design work and present it using clear, accessible language.
