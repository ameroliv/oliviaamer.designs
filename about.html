INTRODUCTION

Hello, my name is Liv!

Hi, I'm Olivia Amer, a web designer and Interaction & Communication Design student at Victoria University. I'm passionate about creating user-centered designs that are intuitive and visually engaging. Currently, I'm in my third year of study and looking forward to new projects and challenging my inner creativity!

When I’m not working on design projects, you can find me listening to music, spending quality time with my friends and family, or going to the gym or on walks. I believe these activities help me recharge and stay inspired, which I then bring into my design work.

Looking ahead, I hope to work on projects that combine cutting-edge design with innovative technologies, contributing to the future of digital experiences.
